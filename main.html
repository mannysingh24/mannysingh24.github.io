My Timeline

March 2022 - April 2022

Database Developer - Nordensight Tech

I did contract work for Nordensight Tech in creating a database full of retail food and environmental health contacts for all western and eastern states, which includes all subdivisions within each state. With the importance of environmental health in our society today, it is absolutely crucial that we know exactly who to contact at any location within the United States to ensure up to date information and promote the safety of the public.

September 2021 - December 2021

Team Leader - The Animal Pad

I helped a nonprofit organization called The Animal Pad (TAP), based here in San Diego, design a database, so they can store important information on their volunteers, dogs, and other personnel. They were using an extensive excel spreadsheet to store information (hard to find and manage information) and had security vulnerabilities, such as information leaking out or certain individuals having access to information that they should not have access to. The database that my team and I designed ensured that security was accounted for and that the TAP workers were able to manage the database with ease. In the end, TAP decided to stick with our database design.
